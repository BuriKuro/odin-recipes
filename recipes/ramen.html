Ramen

Ramen from Japan is one tasty salty dish. It helps cure a sore throat in a pinch.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Mushrooms
  • Boneless skinless chicken breast
  • Salt & pepper
  • White wine
  • Garlic
  • Chicken broth
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame seed oil
  • Ramen noodles
  • Bok choy

Steps

  1. Heat oil and butter. Add the mushrooms.
  2. Slice the chicken breasts.
  3. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  4. Sear the chicken.
  5. Add the wine and let it reduce.
  6. Add butter and garlic.
  7. Add the chicken broth, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  8. Boil the mixture. Add the ramen and bok choy.

Lasagna

Lasagna is delicious! Meat, cheese, sauce, and noodles. How can you not love it? Wow.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion & garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

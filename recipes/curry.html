Curry

Curry from any country is delicious. Enjoy this one from India.

Ingredients

  • Skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • Salt
  • Cooking Oil
  • Chopped onion
  • Minced Garlic
  • Fresh Ginger Root
  • Curry Powder
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Plain yogurt
  • Cilantro
  • Garam masala
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Salt the chicken breasts.
  3. Brown the chicken breasts.
  4. Add onion, garlic, and ginger to the skillet.
  5. Add tomatoes, yogurt, cilantro, and salt to the mixture.
  6. Cook chicken in the mixture with water, garam masala, and more cilantro.
  7. Simmer until chicken is cooked.
